Eligibility Requirements

  • Strong academic performance (minimum B average or equivalent)
  • Demonstrated leadership qualities and community involvement
  • Financial need (priority given to underprivileged students and refugees)
  • Commitment to returning value to your community
  • Currently completing secondary education or recent graduate

We particularly encourage applications from girls, refugees, and students from marginalized communities.

Application Tips

Be Honest

Authenticity matters more than perfection. Share your real story and challenges.

Show Passion

Demonstrate genuine interest in your chosen field and clear goals for the future.

Highlight Impact

Emphasize how education will help you contribute to your community.

Proofread

Check for spelling and grammar errors before submitting your application.
