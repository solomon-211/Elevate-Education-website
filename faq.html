Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our programs, how to get involved, and how we're making an impact.

What is Elevate Education Foundation?

+

Elevate Education Foundation is a nonprofit organization dedicated to bridging the gap in access to quality education in South Sudan and beyond. Founded in 2025, we focus on community learning programs, teacher training, digital resources, scholarship opportunities, and leadership development to empower youth through education, mentorship, and innovation.

When was the foundation established?

+

Elevate Education Foundation was founded in 2025 with a mission to empower youth through education and create lasting change in underserved communities across South Sudan and the surrounding region.

Where are you located and who do you serve?

+

While we focus primarily on South Sudan, we serve communities throughout the region. Our programs are designed to reach both urban and remote areas, with special attention to underserved populations including underprivileged students, refugees, and communities with limited educational resources.

What impact have you made so far?

+

Since our founding, we've achieved significant milestones:

  • Supported over 1,200 students
  • Achieved an 85% improvement in literacy rates
  • Conducted 50 teacher training sessions
  • Trained 500+ youth in digital skills
  • Launched 10 community innovation projects
  • Received recognition from local government for youth empowerment

How is your organization funded?

+

Our funding comes from a diverse mix of sources including individual donations, corporate sponsorships, foundation grants, and partnerships with international development organizations. We maintain financial transparency and ensure that the majority of funds go directly to program implementation.

Are you a registered nonprofit organization?

+

Yes, Elevate Education Foundation is a registered nonprofit organization. We operate with full transparency and accountability, following all legal and regulatory requirements for nonprofit organizations.

What programs do you offer?

+

We offer six comprehensive programs:

  • Community Programs: Local workshops, afterschool support, and learning materials
  • Teacher Training: Professional development for educators
  • Digital Access: Low-cost digital resources and offline materials
  • Scholarship Opportunities: Guidance for talented students seeking university placements
  • Leadership Development: Communication, teamwork, and problem-solving training
  • Coding Programs: Python, JavaScript, and mobile app development courses

Who can benefit from your programs?

+

Our programs serve diverse beneficiaries:

  • Students of all ages seeking quality education
  • Teachers looking for professional development
  • Underprivileged students and refugees seeking scholarships
  • Graduates interested in leadership and technical skills
  • Community members seeking digital literacy

Are your programs free?

+

Many of our community programs and workshops are offered at no cost to participants. Some specialized programs may have nominal fees to cover materials and resources. We're committed to ensuring financial barriers don't prevent access to quality education. Scholarships and financial assistance are available for those who qualify.

What is the Coding and App Development Program?

+

Our coding program offers short-term courses in programming languages including Python and JavaScript, as well as mobile app development. These courses are designed to open career and entrepreneurship opportunities for participants, equipping them with in-demand technical skills. Courses run for 6-12 weeks and include hands-on projects.

Tell me about the Leadership Development Program

+

Our Leadership and Personal Development Programs train graduates in essential skills including communication, teamwork, decision-making, and problem-solving. We focus on building confident leaders who can drive positive change in their communities and professional environments. The program runs for 8-12 weeks and includes interactive workshops, group projects, and community service activities.

What makes your teacher training unique?

+

Our teacher training focuses on practical, applicable skills including lesson planning, classroom management, and assessment techniques. We've conducted over 50 teacher trainings with proven results‚Äî85% of students taught by our trained educators show improved literacy rates. We also provide ongoing mentorship and support throughout the academic year.

Do you offer online or remote programs?

+

Yes! We provide low-bandwidth learning materials and offline-capable resources specifically designed for remote areas with limited internet connectivity. Our digital libraries include printable worksheets and resources that work without constant internet access. We also use solar-powered equipment to ensure accessibility in areas without reliable electricity.

What are After-School Clubs?

+

Our after-school clubs support learners with extra practice in reading and numeracy. These clubs provide a safe, supportive environment where students can get additional help with their studies and engage in educational activities beyond the regular school day. Activities include homework help, tutoring, enrichment activities, and access to library resources.

How long do your programs last?

+

Program durations vary: Community learning centers operate year-round; teacher training workshops run 5-10 days with ongoing support; coding programs are 6-12 weeks; leadership programs are 8-12 weeks; and scholarship guidance is provided throughout the application cycle (typically 6-12 months).

What equipment or materials do participants need?

+

We provide most materials and equipment needed for our programs. For coding programs, we have computer labs available for those without personal devices. Participants typically only need to bring themselves, a willingness to learn, and basic stationery items. We ensure financial and resource barriers don't prevent participation.

How can I apply for a scholarship?

+

We recruit talented underprivileged students and refugees to guide them in securing scholarship opportunities at prestigious universities worldwide. To apply, please visit our Contact page and send us your inquiry including your educational background, academic achievements, and goals. Our team will review your application and guide you through the scholarship process.

How can I volunteer or get involved?

+

We welcome volunteers! You can get involved by:

  • Teaching or mentoring students
  • Helping with community workshops
  • Contributing your professional skills (tech, education, administration)
  • Supporting program coordination
  • Assisting with digital content creation

Visit our Contact page to express your interest, and we'll match you with opportunities that fit your skills and availability.

Can schools partner with you?

+

Absolutely! We welcome partnerships with schools and educational institutions. We can provide teacher training, establish after-school clubs, or set up digital libraries at your institution. Contact us to discuss customized partnership opportunities that align with your school's needs and goals.

How do I stay updated on your work?

+

Stay connected with us through our website and by reaching out via our Contact page. We regularly share updates about our programs, success stories, upcoming events, and opportunities for involvement. You can also follow us on social media platforms for real-time updates.

Do you accept international volunteers?

+

Yes! We welcome volunteers from around the world. International volunteers can contribute remotely (content creation, curriculum development, mentorship) or in-person for longer-term placements. Please contact us with your background, skills, proposed duration, and we'll discuss opportunities and logistics including visa requirements and accommodation.

What are the requirements to volunteer?

+

Requirements vary by role, but generally we look for: commitment to our mission, relevant skills or willingness to learn, minimum time commitment (varies by position), background check for roles involving children, and for teaching roles, relevant educational background or experience. We provide orientation and training for all volunteers.

How can I donate?

+

You can make a donation through our Donate page. We accept various payment methods including credit/debit cards, bank transfers, and mobile money. Every donation, regardless of size, makes a meaningful difference in a student's life. You can make one-time or recurring monthly donations.

How is my donation used?

+

Your donations directly support:

  • Educational materials and digital resources
  • Teacher training workshops and professional development
  • Scholarship funds for deserving students
  • Technology and equipment for coding programs
  • Community learning center operations and maintenance
  • Leadership development programs

We maintain transparency in our financial operations: 75% goes to programs, 15% to operations, and 10% to future growth.

Can organizations sponsor your programs?

+

Yes! Corporate sponsorships and organizational partnerships are vital to expanding our reach. We offer various sponsorship opportunities that can be tailored to your organization's goals, budget, and corporate social responsibility objectives. Benefits include program naming rights, visibility in our materials, and impact reporting. Please reach out via our Contact page to discuss partnership opportunities.

Do you provide tax receipts for donations?

+

As a nonprofit organization, we provide donation receipts for tax purposes where applicable. You will receive an official receipt via email immediately after completing your donation. Please contact us through our Contact page for specific information about tax deductions in your jurisdiction.

Can I donate in-kind instead of money?

+

Yes! We accept in-kind donations of educational materials, computers, tablets, books, school supplies, and other learning resources. Please contact us before sending items to ensure we can accept and properly utilize your donation. We'll provide guidance on what's most needed and arrange logistics.

Is there a minimum donation amount?

+

There is no minimum donation amount. Every contribution, whether $5 or $5,000, helps us continue our work. Even small donations add up and make a real difference when combined with support from our community of donors.

Can I donate in honor or memory of someone?

+

Yes! Tribute gifts are a meaningful way to honor a loved one. When making your donation, you can indicate it's in honor or memory of someone, and we'll send a personalized acknowledgment card to the recipient or family. This is specified in the donation form.

How do I know my donation is secure?

+

We use industry-standard encryption and secure payment processing partners to protect your financial information. We never store your credit card details on our servers. All transactions are processed through trusted, PCI-compliant payment gateways.
