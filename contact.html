Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly will I receive a response?

We aim to respond to all inquiries within 48 hours during business days. For urgent matters, please call our office directly.

Can I visit your offices without an appointment?

While walk-ins are welcome, we recommend scheduling an appointment to ensure someone is available to meet with you and provide the information you need.

Do you accept international volunteers?

Yes! We welcome volunteers from around the world. Please contact us with your background, skills, and proposed duration of volunteer work, and we'll discuss opportunities.

How can schools partner with your organization?

Schools can partner with us for teacher training, after-school programs, digital resource sharing, or scholarship guidance. Contact our partnerships team to explore customized collaboration opportunities.
