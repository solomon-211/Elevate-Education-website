About Elevate Education Foundation

Founded in 2025, Elevate Education Foundation emerged from a profound belief that education is the most powerful tool for transformation in South Sudan and beyond. In regions where conflict, poverty, and limited infrastructure have created significant barriers to learning, we saw an opportunity to make a lasting difference. Our foundation was established by educators, community leaders, and technology innovators who witnessed firsthand the untapped potential of young people yearning for quality education and meaningful opportunities.

Our Mission

To develop and implement innovative educational technologies and community-driven programs that bridge the gap in access to quality education in South Sudan and the surrounding region. We believe every child and young adult deserves the opportunity to learn, grow, and contribute meaningfully to their communities.

Our Vision

A future where geographic location, economic status, or conflict do not determine educational outcomes. We envision thriving communities across South Sudan where learners of all ages have access to world-class educational resources, dedicated teachers, and the digital tools necessary to compete in the global economy.

Our Values

Equity & Inclusion

We prioritize reaching the most marginalized and underserved communities, including refugees, girls, and remote rural populations who have historically been excluded from educational opportunities.

Community Empowerment

Our programs are designed with communities, not for them. We partner with local leaders, parents, and students to ensure our initiatives are culturally relevant and sustainable.

Innovation & Adaptability

We embrace creative solutions to overcome infrastructure challenges, including offline-capable digital resources and mobile learning centers that bring education to learners wherever they are.

Excellence & Impact

We measure our success by the tangible improvements in literacy rates, school enrollment, teacher effectiveness, and the long-term opportunities created for our beneficiaries.

Our Approach

Elevate Education Foundation operates on a holistic model that addresses multiple dimensions of educational access and quality. We recognize that simply building schools is insufficient. Effective education requires trained teachers, engaging materials, supportive learning environments, and pathways to economic opportunity. Our integrated approach includes community learning centers, comprehensive teacher professional development, technology-enabled resources adapted for low-connectivity environments, scholarship guidance for talented students, leadership development programs, and technical skills training in coding and app development.

We work in partnership with local governments, international NGOs, community-based organizations, and private sector partners to maximize our reach and sustainability. Our programs are evidence-based and continuously evaluated to ensure we are achieving meaningful outcomes for the communities we serve.

Key Achievements

  • Established 12 community learning centers across three states in South Sudan
  • Trained over 200 teachers in modern pedagogical approaches and classroom management
  • Supported more than 1,200 students with tutoring, materials, and scholarship guidance
  • Achieved 85% literacy improvement among program participants in pilot communities
  • Trained 500+ youth in digital skills including coding, app development, and computer literacy
  • Launched 10 community-led innovation projects addressing local challenges
  • Secured 45 international scholarship placements for talented underprivileged students
  • Recognized by the South Sudan Ministry of Education for excellence in community education
  • Developed a library of 150+ offline-capable educational resources accessible on low-cost devices
  • Established partnerships with 8 international universities for scholarship pathways

Our Impact Story

When we opened our first learning center in Juba in early 2025, we had 30 students and three volunteer teachers. Today, we serve thousands of learners across multiple locations, and our alumni are studying at universities around the world, teaching in their communities, and starting businesses that create jobs. One of our scholarship recipients, Mary, is now studying computer science at a prestigious university in Kenya and plans to return to develop educational software for South Sudanese students. Stories like Mary's remind us why this work matters and inspire us to reach even more learners.

Looking Forward

As we grow, we remain committed to our founding principles: education should be accessible, relevant, and transformative. Over the next five years, we plan to expand our learning centers to 50 locations, train 1,000 additional teachers, and support 10,000 students annually. We are also developing partnerships to bring solar-powered digital learning labs to remote areas and creating a scholarship fund to ensure our most talented students can access higher education regardless of their economic circumstances.

Join us in this mission. Together, we can elevate education and transform lives across South Sudan and beyond.
