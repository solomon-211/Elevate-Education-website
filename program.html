Our Programs

Elevate Education Foundation offers comprehensive programs designed to address the unique educational challenges facing South Sudan and the region. Each initiative is carefully crafted to create sustainable impact and empower learners at every stage of their educational journey.

Community Learning Centers

Bringing quality education directly to underserved communities

Overview

Our Community Learning Centers serve as educational hubs in areas with limited or no access to formal schooling. These centers provide safe, welcoming spaces where children and adults can engage in structured learning, receive tutoring, and access educational resources that would otherwise be unavailable to them.

What We Offer

  • Afterschool Support Programs: Homework help, tutoring in math and literacy, and enrichment activities for primary and secondary school students
  • Adult Literacy Classes: Reading, writing, and numeracy instruction for adults who missed formal education opportunities
  • Early Childhood Education: Pre-primary learning activities that prepare young children for formal schooling
  • Library Services: Access to age-appropriate books, learning materials, and educational games
  • Community Workshops: Sessions on health, civic education, entrepreneurship, and life skills
  • Parent Engagement Programs: Training for parents on how to support their children's learning at home

Impact Highlights

Since launching our first center in 2025, we have opened 12 locations serving over 1,200 regular learners. Students in our centers have shown remarkable progress, with 85% improving their reading levels by at least two grades within one academic year. Our adult literacy program has enabled 200+ adults to read and write for the first time, opening new economic and civic participation opportunities.

Program Requirements

Our centers are free and open to all community members. Students under 18 require parental consent. We operate Monday through Saturday, with flexible scheduling to accommodate working students and adults.

Teacher Training & Professional Development

Empowering educators to transform classrooms

Overview

Quality education begins with quality teachers. Our comprehensive teacher training program equips educators with modern pedagogical approaches, classroom management strategies, and subject-specific content knowledge. We recognize that many teachers in South Sudan have had limited access to formal teacher education, and our program fills this critical gap.

Training Components

  • Foundational Pedagogy: Child-centered learning approaches, active learning methodologies, and assessment strategies
  • Subject-Specific Training: Deep dives into teaching mathematics, science, languages, and social studies effectively
  • Classroom Management: Creating positive learning environments, managing diverse classrooms, and addressing behavioral challenges
  • Inclusive Education: Strategies for teaching children with disabilities and learning differences
  • Technology Integration: Using digital tools and offline resources to enhance instruction
  • Continuous Professional Support: Ongoing mentorship, peer learning groups, and classroom observation with feedback

Training Format

We offer both intensive workshop sessions (5-10 days) and ongoing support throughout the academic year. Our training is highly practical, with teachers practicing new techniques, receiving feedback, and adapting strategies to their specific contexts. We also provide teaching and learning materials to help teachers implement what they learn.

Impact Highlights

We have trained over 200 teachers across South Sudan, with participants reporting increased confidence, improved student engagement, and better learning outcomes. Follow-up assessments show that 92% of trained teachers are actively implementing new strategies in their classrooms. Teachers also report feeling more connected to a professional community through our ongoing support networks.

Eligibility

Our training is open to current teachers in government and community schools, aspiring teachers, and education coordinators. No prior formal teacher training is required, though participants should have basic literacy and numeracy skills.

Digital Access & Technology Programs

Bridging the digital divide in low-connectivity environments

Overview

Access to digital resources and technology skills is increasingly essential for economic participation and lifelong learning. However, South Sudan faces significant infrastructure challenges including limited internet connectivity and electricity. Our digital access program is specifically designed to work in these constrained environments, using offline-capable resources and solar-powered equipment.

Program Elements

  • Offline Digital Libraries: Pre-loaded tablets and portable servers with thousands of educational resources including textbooks, videos, and interactive lessons
  • Basic Computer Literacy: Foundational digital skills training for students and adults with no prior computer experience
  • Solar-Powered Learning Labs: Mobile computer labs with solar charging systems that can operate in areas without grid electricity
  • Educational Software: Custom-developed and curated applications for mathematics, science, literacy, and vocational skills
  • Digital Content Creation: Training educators to create and adapt digital learning materials for their specific contexts
  • Maintenance & Sustainability: Technical training for local staff to maintain equipment and troubleshoot issues

Technology Infrastructure

We deploy low-cost, durable devices optimized for challenging environments. Our offline-first approach means students can access quality content without internet connectivity. When connectivity is available, we sync new content and provide access to online resources. All equipment is powered by solar panels, making our solutions sustainable and environmentally friendly.

Impact Highlights

We have established 8 digital learning labs serving 600+ learners monthly. Students report increased engagement with learning and improved performance in subjects like mathematics and science. Our offline library contains over 150 curated educational resources aligned with South Sudan's curriculum. Additionally, 300+ community members have gained basic computer skills through our programs.

Access

Digital learning resources are available at all our Community Learning Centers. We also partner with schools and community organizations to extend access. All programs are free for learners.

Scholarship Opportunities Program

Opening doors to higher education for talented students

Overview

Many talented students in South Sudan and refugee communities have the academic potential to excel at top universities worldwide but lack information, guidance, and resources to access scholarship opportunities. Our Scholarship Opportunities Program identifies high-achieving students from underprivileged backgrounds and provides comprehensive support to help them secure admissions and full scholarships to prestigious universities.

Support Services

  • Talent Identification: Working with schools and communities to identify academically gifted students who demonstrate leadership potential
  • Academic Preparation: Advanced tutoring in core subjects, standardized test preparation (SAT, TOEFL, IELTS), and academic writing skills
  • Application Guidance: One-on-one mentorship through the university application process including essay writing, recommendation letters, and interview preparation
  • Scholarship Research: Identifying suitable scholarship programs and ensuring students meet all eligibility requirements
  • Financial Literacy: Training on managing scholarship funds, budgeting, and financial planning for university life
  • Pre-Departure Orientation: Preparing students for academic and cultural transition to their host countries
  • Alumni Network: Ongoing support and mentorship from previous scholarship recipients

Partner Universities

We have established relationships with universities in Kenya, Uganda, Rwanda, Ghana, United States, Canada, United Kingdom, and other countries that offer need-based scholarships and are committed to diversity. These partnerships help streamline the application process and increase success rates for our students.

Impact Highlights

Since 2025, we have supported 45 students in securing full scholarships to universities across Africa, North America, and Europe. Our scholars are studying diverse fields including engineering, medicine, education, computer science, and international development. Our application success rate is 78%, significantly higher than the average. Many of our alumni return during breaks to mentor younger students and give back to their communities.

Eligibility

Students must demonstrate strong academic performance, leadership qualities, financial need, and commitment to community service. We prioritize refugees, girls, and students from the most marginalized communities. The program is open to students completing secondary education or recent graduates.

Leadership & Personal Development Programs

Building confident, capable leaders for tomorrow

Overview

Academic knowledge alone is insufficient for success in today's world. Our Leadership and Personal Development Programs equip young people with essential soft skills, emotional intelligence, and leadership capabilities that are critical for professional success and community impact. These programs are particularly valuable for graduates entering the job market or pursuing entrepreneurial ventures.

Core Curriculum

  • Communication Skills: Public speaking, professional writing, active listening, and interpersonal communication
  • Teamwork & Collaboration: Group dynamics, conflict resolution, and effective collaboration across diverse teams
  • Critical Thinking & Problem-Solving: Analytical thinking, creative problem-solving, and decision-making frameworks
  • Leadership Fundamentals: Leadership styles, ethical leadership, and leading change in communities and organizations
  • Emotional Intelligence: Self-awareness, self-regulation, empathy, and social skills
  • Project Management: Planning, executing, and evaluating community and professional projects
  • Career Readiness: Resume writing, interview skills, networking, and professional etiquette
  • Civic Engagement: Understanding social issues and developing action plans for community improvement

Program Format

Our leadership programs run for 8-12 weeks with weekly sessions combining interactive workshops, group projects, guest speakers from various professional fields, and community service activities. Participants work in cohorts, allowing for peer learning and network building. Each participant completes a capstone project addressing a community challenge.

Impact Highlights

Over 400 young people have completed our leadership program, with 85% reporting increased confidence in their abilities and clearer career direction. Program graduates have gone on to secure competitive jobs, start social enterprises, and take on leadership roles in their communities. Our alumni have initiated 15 community development projects ranging from youth mentorship programs to environmental conservation initiatives.

Who Should Apply

The program is designed for recent secondary school graduates, university students, young professionals, and anyone seeking to develop leadership capabilities. No prior leadership experience is required, just commitment to personal growth and community service.

Coding & App Development Programs

Unlocking opportunities in the digital economy

Overview

Technology skills are increasingly essential for economic opportunity and innovation. Our Coding and App Development Programs provide practical, hands-on training in programming and software development, specifically designed for beginners with no prior coding experience. These short-term intensive courses equip participants with marketable skills that open doors to freelance work, employment, entrepreneurship, and further study in technology fields.

Course Offerings

  • Introduction to Programming with Python: 8-week course covering programming fundamentals, data structures, algorithms, and practical problem-solving with Python
  • Web Development Fundamentals: 10-week course teaching HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and responsive web design for creating modern websites
  • Mobile App Development: 12-week intensive covering mobile app creation for Android using modern frameworks and tools
  • Database & Backend Development: 8-week course on database design, SQL, and building APIs for web applications
  • UI/UX Design Basics: 6-week program on user interface design principles, user experience research, and prototyping tools
  • Tech Entrepreneurship: 6-week program on turning technical skills into business opportunities, including ideation, MVP development, and pitching

Learning Approach

Our courses emphasize learning-by-doing. Students work on real projects from day one, building a portfolio of work they can showcase to potential employers or clients. We use project-based learning where students create functional applications that solve real problems in their communities. Each course includes collaborative group projects, individual assignments, code reviews, and mentorship from experienced developers.

Career Support

Beyond technical training, we provide career development support including portfolio building, freelancing guidance, interview preparation, and connections to tech companies and startups seeking talent. We also host regular tech meetups and hackathons where students can network, collaborate, and showcase their skills.

Impact Highlights

Over 500 individuals have completed our coding programs, with 65% securing tech-related employment or freelance work within 6 months of graduation. Our alumni have developed mobile apps addressing local challenges, launched tech startups, and contributed to open-source projects. Several graduates have advanced to more specialized training programs and university computer science degrees. The average income increase for program completers is 180%.

Prerequisites

Participants need basic computer literacy, access to a computer (we provide lab access for those without personal devices), and strong commitment to learning. No prior programming experience is required. We offer beginner, intermediate, and advanced tracks based on experience level.

Cost

All courses are offered at subsidized rates to ensure accessibility. Scholarships are available for talented students who demonstrate financial need. We also offer income-share agreements where students pay after securing employment.

Get Involved in Our Programs

Whether you want to learn, volunteer as an instructor, or support our programs financially, there are many ways to engage with our work.
